Creamy chicken pasta

Description

Creamy chicken pasta is easily prepared like pasta Alfredo. Straighten with pre-seasoned garlic cream and top with parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 package (250g) of unseasoned chicken fillet strips
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1 jar (2dl) of garlic cream
  • 2 deciliters of grated parmesan
  • 1/2 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 bag (500g) of tagliatelle
  • 1/2 deciliter of chopped parsley

Steps

  1. Bring the chickens to room temperature about 30 minutes before cooking. Brown the chicken fillet strips in oil in a pan. Season with salt and black pepper.
  2. Add cream, parmesan and lemon juice. Bring to a boil. Allow the sauce to simmer over low heat for a few minutes.
  3. Cook the pasta according to the instructions on the package in water seasoned with salt.
  4. Save some of the pasta cooking water. Drain the pasta and mix with the sauce. If necessary, add a little boiling water. Finish with grated parmesan and parsley leaves.
