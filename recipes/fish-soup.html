Fish soup

Description

Easy fish soup is prepared using frozen food. Salmon cubes, soup roots and onion cubes speed up food preparation.

Ingredients

  • 1 package of fish cubes (frozen)
  • 1 deciliter onion cubes (frozen)
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 5 deciliters of water
  • 6 potatoes
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 6 whole black peppercorns
  • 3 deciliters of milk
  • 1 deciliter of chopped dill

Steps

  1. Saute the onion cubes in fat in a pot. Peel and dice the potatoes.
  2. Add water, potatoes, soup roots and salt and pepper to the pot. Cook for about 10 minutes.
  3. Add the iced fish cubes to the pot and cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Finally, measure in the milk and heat until boiling. Add the dill. Serve the soup with bread.
