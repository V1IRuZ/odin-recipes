Meatballs

Description

This recipe makes perfect meatballs! Everyday food is prepared easily and quickly with onion soup ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 600g minced meat
  • 57g/1pc ingredients for onion soup
  • 1/2 deciliter of breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 container (200g) lactose-free cream cheese or 1 1/2 deciliters of water
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Mix the onion soup ingredients, breadcrumbs, pepper, sour cream or water and egg together. Let it swell for 10 minutes.
  2. Add the minced meat and knead in the minced meat until smooth.
  3. Shape the dough into meatballs with hands moistened with water
  4. Bake in the oven at 225 degrees for about 15 minutes. You can also fry the meatballs until cooked in oil in a pan.
